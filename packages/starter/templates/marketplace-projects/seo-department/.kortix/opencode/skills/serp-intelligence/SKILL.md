# SERP Intelligence

Use this skill for market monitoring, competitor checks, brand-term protection,
SERP feature analysis, and opportunity alerts.

## SERP snapshot

Record the basics:

- topic or query set,
- market, language, and device,
- check date,
- target domain,
- competitor domains,
- visible SERP features,
- pages that gained or lost attention.

## Analysis

Separate these forces:

- **Rank movement**: pages changed position for similar SERP layout.
- **Layout movement**: SERP features changed, reducing or increasing organic
  opportunity.
- **Intent movement**: Google appears to prefer a different page type or answer
  depth.
- **Competitor movement**: a competitor improved page quality, freshness,
  authority, internal links, media, schema, or offer.
- **Brand risk**: competitor ads, negative results, outdated profiles, wrong
  sitelinks, missing entity information, or confusing snippets.

## Response options

Recommend exactly one primary response:

- technical fix,
- content refresh,
- new content,
- internal-link push,
- snippet/title/meta test,
- watch-only.

## Output

Return a concise alert with:

- what changed,
- evidence and date,
- why it matters,
- recommended response,
- owner,
- next check.
